FAQ

Questions people ask when a form starts judging them.

How accurate is this teardown?

Directional, not precise. It is based on intake best practices, not a deep audit of your specific form. Think of it as a structured gut check.

Is this for all businesses?

No. It is specifically built for consulting firms with inbound lead flows. If you are not capturing leads through a form, this will be less useful to you.

Does this replace a full audit?

No. The Speed-to-Lead Audit gives you a fuller picture, including scoring, benchmarks, and a structured remediation plan. This teardown is the fast, free entry point.

What if our form is already good?

Still worth running it. Even good forms have gaps that are easy to miss when you are close to them. The tool is free, so there is no downside in checking.
