Common questions

Here is the short version of what firms usually want to know before they book.

Is this for firms that need more leads?

Usually not. The sprint is for firms that already get opportunities but lose too many of them to slow follow up, weak intake, and messy routing.

What if we need broader automation help too?

That is normal. The point is to start with the workflow closest to revenue first, prove value quickly, then expand into the next operational bottleneck.

What makes this different from hiring an implementer?

You are not hiring someone to blindly build whatever you guessed at. Tyler owns the workflow design and implementation, which is the part most firms get wrong.

What happens after the sprint?

If the first workflow win holds, Animas AI can keep improving adjacent workflows like proposal and onboarding, reporting and communication, and internal handoffs.
